Lentils Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Goya Adobo
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3/4ths lb Lentils, rinsed
  • 1 tsp Onion Powder
  • 1 boullion cube
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 4 cups of water

Steps to Make

  1. In a medium saucepan, sautee garlic in low heat until a light brown.
  2. Add water, lentils, boullion, onion powder, adobo, and cumin and mix together.
  3. Bring to a boil, cover, and reduce heat to a simmer.
  4. Cook for 15-20 minutes or until lentils are soft.
