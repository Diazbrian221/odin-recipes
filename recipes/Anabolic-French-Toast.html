Anabolic French Toast By Greg Doucette

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices Sara Lee honey wheat bread (or low calorie sliced bread alternative)
  • 3/4ths of a cup of liquid egg whites (If using regular eggs, add water to thin)
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 packets of splenda (add more if desired)
  • Pam spray or oil spray alternative

Steps to make

  1. Take the egg whites and put it in a mixing bowl. Add cinnamon, vanilla extract, and splenda packets and whisk together.
  2. On a medium-low heated skillet, spray your oil just enough so that it covers the whole pan.
  3. Take your bread slices and soak them in the egg white mixture, flip so each side has absorbed the mixture.
  4. Place bread slices on the skillet. Pour any of the remaining mixture on the bread slices slowly.
  5. After 2-3 minutes, flip bread slices.
  6. Cook until both sides are firm and golden-dark brown.
  7. Serve with low calorie syrup and/or berries and enjoy!
